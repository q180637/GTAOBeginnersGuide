Overview

Cocaine Lockups are one of the five passive businesses available for purchase as an MC President. To register as an MC President the player needs to purchase one of the 12 clubhouses available around the map. This will then allow them to become an MC President in the interaction menu and access the Open Road website from the laptop in their office. On the Open Road website the player can choose to purchase a Cocaine Lockup in one of 4 locations: Alamo Sea, Paleto Bay, Elysian Island or Morningwood which range in price from GTA$975,000 to GTA$1,852,500. As the Cocaine Lockup is a passive business it must be frequently restocked with supplies that can then be turned into a sellable product. The rate at which supplies are consumed and the value of the product produced can be improved through purchasing upgrades from the laptop inside the Cocaine Lockup.

Sourcing Supplies

Like all of the MC businesses there are two ways that the player can source supplies for the Cocaine Lockup: stealing supplies or buying them. Stealing supplies can be started from the Open Road website on the laptop inside the Cocaine Lockup and then navigating to the Resupply page and choosing Steal Supplies. This will start a short mission in Free Mode that tasks the player with locating, collecting and returning "Supplies" back to the Cocaine Lockup. Upon starting one of these missions the game will choose one of 10 missions to give the player some of which are as simple as collecting a vehicle or bag with others having some minor mechanics involved. The majority of these missions are shared with the other 4 MC businesses with the exception of one: Disrupt a Humane Labs operation. This unique mission has the player go to a some crashed Humane Labs Boxville vans near the railway, defeat some enemies that are defending them and return the Boxville to the Cocaine Lockup. Stealing supplies generates 1 unit of supplies per member of the Motorcycle Club with each player only being able to collect 1 unit before having to return them to the business.

Alternatively, the player could choose to buy supplies on the same Open Road resupply page. This will cost the player between GTA$15,000 and GTA$75,000 depending on how many units of supplies are purchased. While this option allows the player to partake in Grand Theft Auto Online's other profiteering activites by skipping a potentially time consuming mission it will cut into their profits. Should the player choose to buy supplies as their resupply option the player will receive a notification informing them that the supplies are on the way to the business and will arrive shortly. Roughly 10 minutes later the business' supplies bar will be completely filled up from whatever level it was previously at.

Upon collecting any amount of supplies the business will begin to produce product over time. Without any business upgrades the Cocaine Lockup will require the player to have the business producing product for 8 hours 20 minutes in order to have a full bar of product. This time can be reduced to 5 hours by purchasing the Staff and Equipment upgrades from the Open Road laptop inside the Cocaine Lockup which will cost a total of GTA$1,325,000.

Selling Product

After any amount of product has been generated the player can visit the Open Road website from the laptop inside their Cocaine Lockup and choose to sell their product to either a local buyer in the same county as the business or a remote buyer further afield. Like all other MC businesses there are 7 possible sell missions which the player could be given and are differentiated by the delivery vehicle the player has to use. Some of the missions also have certain challenges that the player has to handle during the sale as well such as flying a Dodo seaplane at low level to five aerial drop locations whereas others are as simple as delivering a Western Bagger to a location. There is one sell mission unique to the Cocain Lockup that is functionally similar to the standard Western Bagger delivery with a slight change. This change is that the sale was a Police Setup and therefore the player will have to lose a wanted level then deliver the Bagger to a secondary drop location which will be sent to them by LJT.

The highest amount of GTA$ a player can earn from a single sale in this business is GTA$504,000 on a remote sale with both the Staff and Equipment upgrades being purchased. If the player chooses to only purchase supplies when the business has none remaining to fill up their Cocaine Lockup it will cost them GTA$225,000 making this the most profitable MC business with a maximum full sale profit of GTA$279,000. Please note that these values are calculated with the assumption that no supplies are remaining from previous production cycles and does not account for the player being able to make money with other methods while the business is producing.
